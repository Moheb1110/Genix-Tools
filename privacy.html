🛡️ Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. No tracking, no storage.

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

1. No Data Collection

At Genix Tools, we believe your data belongs to you. All our tools (Word Counter, BMI Calculator, etc.) are client-side. This means all processing happens directly in your browser. We do not store, save, or transmit any text or data you enter into our tools to any server.

2. Cookies and Analytics

We use standard tools like Google Search Console to monitor our website's performance. These tools may use "cookies" to collect anonymous information such as:

  • Browser type and version.
  • The pages you visit on our site.
  • Time spent on those pages.

3. Local Storage

Some tools may use your browser's "Local Storage" to save your preferences (like dark mode or your last typing score) locally on your device. This data never leaves your computer.

4. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to other websites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact the developer through our official GitHub repository or social media channels.
